Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 14, 2026

1. Introduction

Softroni LLC ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our websites, mobile applications, and services (collectively, the "Services").

This policy covers all current and future Softroni applications. It is organized by data category so you can quickly understand what applies to the apps you use.

2. Offline-First Design

Our applications are designed to work offline whenever possible. Most core functionality operates entirely on your device with no internet connection required. Data is transmitted to external servers only when strictly necessary — for example, subscription verification via Apple, optional crash reporting, or features that explicitly require cloud processing (such as AI-generated content).

We minimize data collection by default. If an app does not need your data to function, we do not collect it.

3. General Data

Across all Softroni applications, we may collect the following:

  • On-device preferences: Settings, session history, and user progress are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.
  • Purchases and subscriptions: In-app purchases and subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple. We receive a purchase receipt to verify your subscription status but do not receive your payment details (credit card, billing address, etc.).
  • Crash reports: Anonymous crash logs may be collected via Apple's built-in crash reporting to help us diagnose and fix bugs. These contain no personally identifiable information.
  • Analytics: We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics (e.g., which features are used most often) to improve our apps. This data cannot be used to identify individual users.
  • Support communications: If you contact us for support, we retain the information you provide for as long as necessary to resolve your request.

Most of our applications do not require an account, login, or any personal information to use.

4. Health & Fitness Data

Some Softroni applications may collect health and fitness data to provide their core functionality. When applicable:

  • Motion and sensor data: Data from device sensors, AirPods motion sensors, or Apple Watch may be used for real-time analysis (e.g., posture detection, activity tracking). All processing is performed on-device. Raw sensor data is not stored beyond the active session.
  • Camera-based analysis: When camera-based features are enabled, video frames are processed on-device in real time. Frames are never recorded, stored, or transmitted to any server.
  • Apple Health (HealthKit): If you grant permission, our apps may read from or write to Apple Health. This data stays within the Apple Health ecosystem and is governed by Apple's privacy policies. We never access HealthKit data without your explicit consent, and we never send HealthKit data to our servers or any third party.

Health and fitness features always require your explicit permission before accessing any sensor, camera, or HealthKit data. You can revoke these permissions at any time in your device's Settings.

5. Children's Data (COPPA Compliance)

Certain Softroni applications may be designed for use by children under the supervision of a parent or guardian. For any app that collects data from or about children under 13, we comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and take the following measures:

  • Parental consent required: A parent or guardian must set up the app and consent to the collection of any child-related data before a child can use it.
  • Minimal data collection: We collect only the minimum information necessary for the app to function — such as a child's first name or age range for content personalization. We do not collect email addresses, photos, precise location, or any other personal information from children.
  • No advertising to children: Apps designed for children contain no advertisements of any kind. We do not serve ads, display sponsored content, or use any ad tracking in apps intended for children.
  • No behavioral tracking: We do not track, profile, or build behavioral models of children. No child data is shared with third parties for any purpose.
  • Parental controls: Parents can review, modify, or delete all child-related data directly within the app at any time. Parents may also request complete deletion of any data by contacting us through our Support page.
  • Data deletion: All child-related data can be deleted by the parent through the app's settings. If cloud processing was used, server-side data is automatically deleted within 24 hours of processing.

6. Audio & Voice Data

Some Softroni applications may process audio or voice data. When applicable:

  • Voice recordings: If an app offers voice-based features (such as voice cloning or voice input), recordings are initiated and controlled by the user (or parent, in the case of children's apps). Voice data is stored on-device by default.
  • Cloud voice processing: If cloud processing is required to create a voice model, recordings are encrypted in transit, processed on our secure servers, and deleted within 24 hours. We do not retain voice data beyond what is necessary for processing.
  • Audio preferences: Sound settings, playback preferences, and audio library selections are stored on-device only and are never transmitted to our servers.
  • Children's voice protections: In apps designed for children, only a parent's voice is recorded (if the feature exists). A child's voice is never recorded or processed.

7. AI-Powered Features

Some Softroni applications may use artificial intelligence to generate or personalize content. When applicable:

  • Content generation: Minimal input data (such as a name or preference) may be sent to our AI processing service to generate personalized content. These inputs are encrypted in transit and are not stored after content generation is complete.
  • On-device AI: Where possible, AI processing is performed entirely on your device using on-device models, with no data sent to external servers.
  • No training on your data: We do not use your personal data, voice recordings, or content preferences to train AI models.

8. No Third-Party Ad Tracking

All Softroni applications are ad-free. We do not display advertisements, use ad SDKs, or share any data with advertising networks. We do not participate in ad tracking, retargeting, or data brokerage programs. This applies to all our apps, including those designed for children.

9. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our applications and services.
  • Process subscriptions and in-app purchases via Apple.
  • Generate personalized content when you use AI-powered features.
  • Diagnose and fix crashes and technical issues.
  • Respond to your support requests.

We do not use your data for targeted advertising, behavioral profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the app's core functionality.

10. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data only in these circumstances:

  • Apple: Subscription and purchase verification is handled by Apple's App Store infrastructure.
  • Cloud processing: When cloud-based features are used (e.g., AI content generation, voice processing), minimal data is sent to our processing service, encrypted in transit, and deleted after processing.
  • Crash reporting: Anonymous crash data may be collected via Apple's crash reporting framework.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose data if required by law, regulation, or legal process.

11. Data Retention

Most data in our apps is stored locally on your device and persists only as long as the app is installed. For data that passes through our servers:

  • AI and voice processing data is deleted within 24 hours of processing.
  • Crash reports are retained for up to 90 days to diagnose issues.
  • Support communications are retained for as long as necessary to resolve your request.

When data is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it.

12. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest, regular security assessments, and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

13. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • Object to or restrict the processing of your data.
  • Request portability of your data.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

Because most of our apps store data only on your device, you can exercise these rights directly by managing or deleting the app. For any server-side data, please contact us through our Support page.

14. Third-Party Links

Our Services may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

15. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, including when we release new applications. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on our website with a revised "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of our Services after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

16. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, our data practices, or wish to exercise your rights regarding your personal data, please reach out through our Support page.
