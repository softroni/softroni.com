FAQ

Common questions

Is it really free?

Yes — 100% free, forever. No paywall, no trial, no in-app purchases. We believe focus tools should be accessible to everyone.

Do I need to be on camera?

No. Beside uses ambient scenes — animated environments with presence cues — not real people. Your camera is never used.

What is body doubling?

Body doubling is a focus technique especially popular in the ADHD community. It's the simple idea that having another person present — even silently — makes it easier to start and sustain tasks. Beside recreates that feeling through ambient scenes. Read our full guide →

Is my data private?

Yes. No account required. Everything stays on your device. We don't collect usage data, and there are no analytics or trackers in the app.
