FAQ

Common questions

Is TorqueLog really free?

Yes. The free version includes 1 vehicle with full service logging, fuel tracking, smart reminders, receipt photos, and iCloud sync — with no ads. Pro unlocks unlimited vehicles, cost analytics, and PDF export.

Does my data sync across devices?

Yes. TorqueLog uses Apple's CloudKit to sync your data across all your iCloud-connected devices automatically. No account or sign-up needed.

What kinds of services can I track?

Anything — oil changes, tire rotations, brake pads, battery replacements, inspections, fluid flushes, and more. You can also create custom service types for anything specific to your vehicle.

Can I track fuel economy?

Yes. Log each fill-up with the amount, price, and odometer reading. TorqueLog calculates your MPG (or L/100km) automatically and shows trends over time.

Is my data private?

Completely. No accounts, no tracking, no analytics. Your data is stored on-device and in your personal iCloud. We never see it. Read our privacy policy →
