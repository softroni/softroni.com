Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 14, 2026

1. Introduction

Softroni LLC ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our websites, mobile applications, and services (collectively, the "Services").

This policy covers all Softroni apps, including but not limited to: Beside, SitTall, SleepyFable, ColorLull, bloomink, and MuteWave. As we release new apps, they are governed by this same policy unless stated otherwise.

2. Offline-First Design

Our apps are designed to work offline whenever possible. Most core functionality — including focus sessions, sound playback, coloring, and posture reminders — operates entirely on your device with no internet connection required.

Data transmission occurs only when necessary for specific features: subscription verification via Apple, optional crash reporting, and features that explicitly require cloud processing (such as AI-generated content). We minimize data collection by default.

3. Information We Collect

The data we collect depends on which app you use and which features you enable. Here is what each category of app may collect:

3a. Minimal-Data Apps (Beside, ColorLull/bloomink, MuteWave)

  • No account required. These apps do not require sign-up or login.
  • On-device preferences: Session history, settings, and progress are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.
  • Subscription data: If you make an in-app purchase, the transaction is processed entirely by Apple. We receive a purchase receipt to verify your subscription status but do not receive your payment information.
  • Crash reports: Anonymous crash logs may be collected via Apple's built-in crash reporting to help us fix bugs. These contain no personally identifiable information.

3b. Health & Fitness Data (SitTall)

  • Posture data: SitTall uses AirPods motion sensors, device camera, and/or Apple Watch to detect posture. All posture analysis is performed on-device in real time.
  • Health data: If you grant permission, SitTall may read or write posture-related data to Apple Health (HealthKit). This data stays within the Apple Health ecosystem and is never sent to our servers.
  • Camera data: When camera-based posture detection is enabled, video frames are processed on-device in real time and are never recorded, stored, or transmitted.
  • Motion data: AirPods motion data is processed on-device and is not stored beyond the active session.

3c. AI-Powered Features (SleepyFable)

  • Story preferences: Child's name and age may be provided by the parent to personalize bedtime stories. This data is stored on-device and used only for story generation.
  • Voice data: SleepyFable's voice cloning feature allows a parent to record their voice for narration. Voice recordings are processed to create a voice model. Voice data is stored on-device unless cloud processing is required, in which case it is encrypted in transit, processed, and deleted from our servers within 24 hours.
  • AI-generated content: Story prompts may be sent to our AI processing service to generate personalized stories. These prompts contain the child's name and age but no other identifying information. Prompts are not stored after story generation is complete.

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

SleepyFable is designed for use by children under the supervision of a parent or guardian. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and take the following measures:

  • Parental consent required: A parent or guardian must set up SleepyFable and consent to the collection of any child-related data (name, age) before the child can use the app.
  • Minimal data collection: We collect only the child's first name and age range for story personalization. We do not collect email addresses, photos, location data, or any other personal information from children.
  • No advertising to children: SleepyFable contains no advertisements of any kind. We do not serve ads, display sponsored content, or use any ad tracking in apps designed for children.
  • No behavioral tracking: We do not track, profile, or build behavioral models of children. No child data is shared with third parties for any purpose.
  • Voice data protections: Voice cloning is initiated and controlled by the parent only. The child's voice is never recorded. The parent's voice model is stored on-device and can be deleted at any time from the app's settings.
  • Parental controls: Parents can review, modify, or delete all child data directly within the app. Parents may also request complete deletion of any data by contacting us through our Support page.
  • Data deletion: All child-related data can be deleted at any time by the parent through the app's settings. If cloud processing was used, server-side data is automatically deleted within 24 hours of processing.

5. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our apps and services.
  • Process subscriptions and in-app purchases via Apple.
  • Generate personalized content (e.g., bedtime stories in SleepyFable).
  • Diagnose and fix crashes and technical issues.
  • Respond to your support requests.

We do not use your data for targeted advertising, behavioral profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the app's core functionality.

6. No Third-Party Ad Tracking

All Softroni apps are ad-free. We do not display advertisements, use ad SDKs, or share any data with advertising networks. We do not participate in any ad tracking, retargeting, or data brokerage programs. Period.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data only in these circumstances:

  • Apple: Subscription and purchase verification is handled by Apple's App Store infrastructure.
  • AI processing: When cloud-based AI features are used (e.g., story generation), minimal data is sent to our processing service, encrypted in transit, and deleted after processing.
  • Crash reporting: Anonymous crash data may be collected via Apple's crash reporting framework.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose data if required by law, regulation, or legal process.

8. Data Retention

Most data in our apps is stored locally on your device and persists only as long as the app is installed. For data that passes through our servers:

  • AI processing data (story prompts, voice processing) is deleted within 24 hours of processing.
  • Crash reports are retained for up to 90 days to diagnose issues.
  • Support communications are retained for as long as necessary to resolve your request.

When data is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it.

9. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest, regular security assessments, and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • Object to or restrict the processing of your data.
  • Request portability of your data.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

Because most of our apps store data only on your device, you can exercise these rights directly by managing or deleting the app. For any server-side data, please contact us through our Support page.

11. App-Specific Details

For quick reference, here is what each app collects:

  • Beside (Body Doubling Focus) — No account, no tracking. Session history stored on-device only. Optional subscription via Apple.
  • SitTall (Posture Reminder) — On-device posture analysis via AirPods motion, camera, or Apple Watch. Optional HealthKit integration. No data leaves your device.
  • SleepyFable (AI Bedtime Stories) — Child's name/age for story personalization (parent-consented). Parent voice cloning stored on-device. AI story generation may use cloud processing with data deleted within 24 hours. COPPA compliant. No ads.
  • ColorLull / bloomink (Adult Coloring) — No account, no tracking. All artwork and progress stored on-device only.
  • MuteWave (Brown Noise & Sounds) — No account, no tracking. Sound preferences stored on-device only.

12. Third-Party Links

Our Services may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, including when we release new apps. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on our website with a revised "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of our Services after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

14. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, our data practices, or wish to exercise your rights regarding your personal data, please reach out through our Support page.
